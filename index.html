Banking analytics

Credit Risk & Customer Segmentation Analysis

30K customers analysed
4 risk segments
54% default recall

The problem

Banks need to identify high-risk credit card customers early, understand repayment behaviour, and support better credit decisions with analysis that is both accurate and easy to interpret.

What I built

  • Engineered behavioural features such as utilisation, payment ratio, late payments, maximum delay, and recent usage.
  • Used clustering to group customers into business-relevant segments like high risk, low activity, and high value.
  • Built a predictive model for default risk and improved minority-class detection using class imbalance handling.
  • Designed a Power BI dashboard to present customer behaviour, default status, segment distribution, and repayment patterns.

Business result

The analysis shows that customers with heavy credit usage and repeated payment delays exhibit meaningfully higher default risk, making earlier intervention and more informed credit strategy decisions possible.

AI automation

Country Risk Intelligence System

5 countries monitored
Daily automated briefing cycle
n8n workflow orchestration

The problem

Country-risk monitoring is often slow, manual, and difficult to scale when analysts need consistent updates across multiple markets.

What I built

  • Built an automated workflow that collects news, prepares structured headlines, sends articles to an LLM for risk analysis, and outputs daily briefings.
  • Designed a concise reporting format that includes risk score, stability signal, deal indicators, alerts, regulatory updates, and opportunities.
  • Created a reusable automation pipeline suitable for banking, investment, and market-monitoring use cases.

Business result

The system turns raw market news into a repeatable intelligence product, reducing manual monitoring effort and improving consistency in country-level risk reporting.
